My name is Christoffer Carlsson. I believe that each of us should have the freedom to define life on our own terms.

If you’re curious about the work I’ve done previously you can take a look at my resumé. You’ll also find my open source work over on GitHub.

In my spare time I enjoy learning about obscure programming concepts and tinker with fun side projects. I also enjoy design, photography and good food.
