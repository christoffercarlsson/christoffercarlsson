# Hej!

My name is Christoffer Carlsson. I believe that each of us should have the
freedom to define life on our own terms.

By helping to build tools and exploring strategies that give us flexibility,
privacy, security and simplicity, I hope to contribute to a world where we all
can live independently.

I live in Gothenburg, Sweden, with my partner Cecilia and our two daughters. I
currently work as a Software Engineer at Volvo Cars.

If you’re curious about the work I’ve done previously you can take a look at my
[resumé](/resume). You’ll also find my open source work over on
[GitHub](https://github.com/christoffercarlsson).

In my spare time I enjoy learning about obscure programming concepts and tinker
with fun side projects. I also enjoy design, photography and good food.

If you want to get in touch you can
[send me an email](mailto:cc@christofferc.com) (you can encrypt your message
using my [PGP public key](/cc.asc)).
